Three essential calculators every electrician needs — in one professional app.

CONDUIT FILL CALCULATOR
• Supports EMT, IMC, RMC, PVC-40, PVC-80, FMC, LFMC
• Calculate fill percentage for any wire combination
• Instantly find minimum conduit size required
• Color-coded pass/fail indicators
